## Booking And Deposit Flow

1. Visitor selects event type: Gaming, Movie, Golf, Combo, Corporate, Fundraiser.
2. Visitor enters date, time window, address/city, guest count, age range, and indoor/outdoor parking notes.
3. System asks setup qualifiers:
   - Is there flat parking for a 24-28 ft trailer?
   - Any HOA, venue, or city restrictions?
   - Power available, or should generator be assumed?
   - Any tight driveways, low branches, or slopes?
4. Owner reviews availability and sends quote.
5. Customer accepts package and pays deposit, recommended 25% or $100 minimum.
6. Confirmation email includes arrival window, space requirements, cancellation/weather terms, supervision notes, and movie-license limitations if booking movie mode.
7. Balance due 48 hours before event or on arrival before setup.

Recommended stack:
- Website: Squarespace, Wix, Webflow, or WordPress.
- Scheduler/payment: Acuity, Calendly + Stripe, Square Appointments, or SimplyBook.me.
- CRM/light automation: Google Sheets/Airtable plus Zapier, or HoneyBook/Dubsado if you want contracts and invoices together.
