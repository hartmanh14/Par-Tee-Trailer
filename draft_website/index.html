Questions planners will ask

Clear answers reduce quote friction.

Is this still a three-in-one entertainment trailer?

No. The revised launch plan starts with a golf simulator trailer. Movie and gaming configurations are future options after the golf offer is validated.

How many people can participate?

The trailer is designed for rotating group play. Final capacity depends on trailer size, seating layout, venue rules, and safe swing clearance.

What changes for a fundraiser?

The seating area can be swapped for a sign-in desk and event management hub for donations, turn order, sponsor materials, prizes, and leaderboard tracking.

Can it run without venue power?

The launch build is planned around generator and shore-power flexibility. Generator rules, noise limits, and placement need to be confirmed with each venue.
