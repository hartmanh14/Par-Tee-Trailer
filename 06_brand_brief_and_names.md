# Brand Brief And Naming Options

## Positioning

A premium but approachable mobile entertainment trailer for Troy and the Dayton metro. It brings golf, movies, and console gaming to backyards, businesses, schools, fundraisers, tailgates, and private events.

The brand should feel energetic, clean, local, and trustworthy. It should avoid sounding like only a kids' game truck or only a golf simulator business.

## Target Audiences

- Parents booking birthdays and teen parties.
- Adults booking golf nights, bachelor parties, tailgates, and family events.
- HR, office managers, and corporate event planners.
- Schools, churches, PTAs, HOAs, apartment communities, breweries, and fundraisers.

## Promise

One trailer. Three experiences. Delivered to your event.

## Tone

- Confident and clear.
- Fun without being childish.
- Professional enough for corporate buyers.
- Local enough for Troy/Dayton community buyers.

## Name And Tagline Options

| Option | Name | Tagline | Best For |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | Triple Play Mobile Entertainment | Golf. Movies. Gaming. Delivered. | Clearest all-in-one positioning |
| 2 | Rally Trailer Co. | The party pulls up. | Broad, energetic brand with room to expand |
| 3 | The Event Bay | Private golf, movies, and gaming on wheels. | Premium/corporate-friendly positioning |
| 4 | Swing Screen Play | Three ways to play, one mobile venue. | Memorable, concept-specific |
| 5 | OnDeck Mobile Lounge | Golf nights, movie nights, game nights. Anywhere. | Lifestyle/event lounge feel |

Recommended first choice: Triple Play Mobile Entertainment.

Why: It instantly communicates the three-mode concept, works for kids and adults, and does not require much explanation in ads or marketplace listings.

## Visual Direction

- Logo: bold wordmark plus simple three-icon system: golf flag/club, play button/screen, controller.
- Colors: charcoal/black base, electric green accent for golf/energy, warm red or amber accent for movie/game warmth, clean white text.
- Trailer wrap: large readable name, "Golf Simulator | Movie Theater | Gaming Lounge", phone, URL, QR code, service radius, and strong side-panel action photo/render.
- Avoid: overly cartoon gaming graphics, golf-only country-club styling, dark unreadable wrap art, tiny service descriptions.

## Core Message Blocks

Primary headline:
Golf, movies, and gaming delivered to your event.

Short description:
A climate-controlled mobile entertainment trailer for birthdays, corporate events, fundraisers, tailgates, school events, and private parties across Troy and the Dayton metro.

Offer stack:
- Mobile golf simulator parties
- Private movie nights
- Multi-console gaming lounge
- Combo packages
- Corporate and fundraiser events

Proof to collect during launch:
- Photos/video of all three modes
- First 10 reviews
- Changeover under 20 minutes
- COI-ready insurance
- Safe power/generator setup

## Brand Guardrails

- Always mention all three modes in first-touch marketing.
- Use "climate-controlled" and "we come to you" often.
- For corporate outreach, lead with team-building and employee appreciation.
- For parent audiences, lead with easy party hosting and supervised gaming.
- For golf audiences, lead with private, convenient, all-weather play.
