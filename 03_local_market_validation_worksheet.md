## Desk-Research Conclusions

- The Dayton market already has mobile gaming and outdoor movie substitutes, so this should not be pitched as a totally novel category.
- The differentiation is the all-in-one, climate-controlled trailer: gaming for birthdays, golf for adults/corporate, movie for evenings and families.
- Fixed-site golf simulator pricing appears far below mobile event pricing, so mobile golf must sell convenience, private experience, and group/event energy rather than hourly practice alone.
- Public pricing is inconsistent; many vendors hide pricing behind booking forms. Direct quote shopping is a Phase 0 must-do.
- The climate supports year-round indoor mobile entertainment, especially winter, rain, humid summer days, and shoulder-season events.
